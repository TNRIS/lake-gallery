Lake Story Map - Mobile Instructions

Important: The lake story map requires the use of two fingers to pan and zoom around the lake area on a mobile device.

The map has restricted bounds based on each lakes' boundary. The map also has restricted minimum zoom (how far you can zoom out).

To view the available tools in a lake story view, click the tools menu icon in the top right of the map.

In the tools menu under the time slider, some lakes have hyperlinked years that will send you to that specific historical imagery collection via the TNRIS DataHub. To view an available historical imagery collection for a lake story:

  1. Open the tools menu in the top right of the story map by clicking/touching the menu icon.
  2. Click/touch a year on the time slider to overlay a historical imagery layer to the map.
  3. Click/touch the same year again in the time slider. If there is an available collection for that year, a new browser tab will open and send you to that historical imagery page on the DataHub.

For any other questions or concerns, please read through the Lakes of Texas About page and use the Contact form if you would like to reach out to TNRIS.
